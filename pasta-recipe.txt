Ingredients:
 - 2 cloves garlic, chopped
 - Six 8-ounce cans tomato sauce
 - One 2-ounce can tomato paste
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
 - 2 pounds of your favorite pasta
 

Directions:
 1. Place the six 8-ounce cans of tomato sauce and one 2-ounce can tomato paste in a large pot on low simmer
 2. Put in the salt and black pepper as well as the chopped garlic
 3. Mix well and let sit for 3 hours 
 4. In another large pot, boil water
 5. Place the 2 pounds of pasta in the boiling water
 6. When the pasta is al dente mix the pasta with appropriate amount of sauce 
 7. Serve with parmesean cheese
